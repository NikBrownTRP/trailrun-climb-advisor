Climb Advisor — the living hillside

The screen is your effort zone. A slope steepens as the live gradient rises, and the zone word — RUN → POWER-HIKE → HIKE — rides up that slope, tilting with the gradient and recolouring green → orange → red. Stats sit quietly on the edges.

STATESThe word climbs the hill

No figure — the zone word itself is the graphic. It sits on the slope and rotates with the live gradient, so a glance reads both how steep and what to do. Grade (cyan) tops the dial; heart rate sits at the foot and turns red over threshold.

What translates to the watch: black background, the slope as a filled canvas path and the zone word drawn with ctx.rotate + fillText in zone hex — both recomputed each evaluate() tick from mode and gradePct. Grade/HR come from the existing script/keyValue formatters. CSS chrome, web fonts and the slider UI are mock-only; tune the real word/slope exaggeration on-device.
